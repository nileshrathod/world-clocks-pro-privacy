FAQ

  • New Tab override? World Clocks Pro does not override Chrome’s New Tab. You can open the full dashboard from the toolbar or enable “Open on startup” in Settings → Behavior.
  • Backup & Restore? Use Export Settings / Import in Settings to transfer your setup to another computer.
  • Where are my settings stored? In Chrome’s local storage and optionally Chrome Sync, on your device.
