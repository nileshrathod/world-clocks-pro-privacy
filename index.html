World Clocks Pro – Privacy Policy

Last updated: Aug 11, 2025

Overview

World Clocks Pro is a Chrome extension that provides a customizable world-clock dashboard (popup and new-tab) with digital and analog clocks.

We respect your privacy. This extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with anyone.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect personal information and does not send any data to external servers. All preferences are stored locally in your browser and may sync with your Google account via Chrome Sync if you have it enabled.

  • Saved data may include: selected cities/time zones, layout/size positions, theme and icon preferences.

Permissions Used

  • storage – to save your settings (cities, layout, theme) locally and via Chrome Sync.
  • chrome_url_overrides → newtab – to show the clocks dashboard as your New Tab page (core functionality).

No other permissions are requested.

Cookies & Tracking

The extension does not set cookies and does not use analytics or tracking technologies.

Third Parties

No third-party services are integrated. The extension does not communicate with any external API.

Children’s Privacy

The extension does not target children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If the policy changes, an updated version will be published on this page. Your continued use of the extension after changes means you accept the updated policy.
